About Me

Sensor development engineer with a PhD from IIT Madras (Best Thesis Award) and 2.5 years of industrial R&D at Saint-Gobain Research India (Life Sciences – Sensors Division), where I have been recognised twice as Most Valuable Professional (2024 & 2025).

My expertise bridges MEMS sensor design, flexible electronics, nanomaterial synthesis, and bioprocess pressure sensing. I take ideas from first principles through to cGMP-compliant validated products — combining deep materials science with hands-on embedded electronics and data-driven optimisation.

I have led projects spanning single-use pharmaceutical bioprocess sensors, a 10 million Euro e-vehicle thermal valve system, acoustic high-temperature sensors, and conductive ceramic coatings — always working at the intersection of cutting-edge materials and practical engineering.

Actively seeking roles in sensor development, R&D engineering, or materials innovation worldwide — Europe, USA, and Asia-Pacific.

Key Projects

Single-Use Pressure Sensors for Bioprocess

Spearheaded development of sterile single-use pressure sensors for pharmaceutical bioprocess manufacturing, applying MEMS design, flexible substrate engineering, and cGMP compliance for cell culture monitoring and downstream processing.

MEMSBioprocesscGMPLife Sciences

Thermal Management Valve for E-Vehicle

Led cross-functional team on an electronic thermal valve actuation system for e-vehicle deployment — managing technical design, embedded electronics integration, and stakeholder coordination from concept through production validation.

Embedded SystemsAutomotiveProject Mgmt

Flexible Strain Sensor Tape (Patented)

Invented polymer nanocomposite-based flexible strain sensor adhesive tape using polymer-functionalized CNT/graphene. Granted Indian patent for novel technology enabling wearable electronics and structural health monitoring.

Flexible ElectronicsCNT/GrapheneWearablesPatented

NOx Gas Sensor from Biomass

Pioneered environmentally sustainable NO₂ gas sensor using biomass-derived carbon from invasive Prosopis Juliflora, demonstrating a novel materials science approach addressing both sensing performance and ecological challenges.

Gas SensorsSustainabilityMetal Oxides

Piezoresistive MEMS Accelerometer

Architected MEMS acceleration sensor with zero cross-axis sensitivity through advanced FEM optimisation (COMSOL Multiphysics) and integrated silicon nanowire piezoresistor design, achieving high prime-axis response without sacrificing bandwidth.

MEMSFEM / COMSOLNanowire

Embedded Electronics for Sensors

Designed and deployed portable embedded electronics interface systems including LabVIEW-based test stations, IoT-enabled wireless acquisition, real-time signal processing, and sensor-to-system integration for industrial and wearable applications.
