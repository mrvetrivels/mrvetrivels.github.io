Key Projects

Single-Use Pressure Sensors for Bioprocess

Developed pressure sensors specifically designed for single-use systems in pharmaceutical bioprocess applications, addressing sterility and cost-effectiveness requirements.

MEMS Bioprocess cGMP Life Sciences

10M Euro E-Vehicle Thermal Valve System

Led cross-functional team developing electronic thermal valve actuating system for electric vehicles, from concept to deployment, managing technical and business stakeholders.

Embedded Systems Automotive Project Management

Flexible Strain Sensor (Patented)

Developed polymer nanocomposite-based flexible strain sensor adhesive tape using CNT/graphene functionalization, achieving high sensitivity and durability for wearable and structural health monitoring.

Flexible Electronics Nanomaterials Wearables CNT/Graphene

NOx Gas Sensor from Biomass

Developed environmentally sustainable NO2 gas sensor using biomass carbon derived from invasive plant species (Prosopis Juliflora), demonstrating climate-positive innovation.

Gas Sensors Sustainability Metal Oxides

MEMS Accelerometer Design

Designed piezoresistive MEMS acceleration sensor with zero cross-axis sensitivity and high prime-axis response without sacrificing operational bandwidth, using FEM optimization.

MEMS FEM (COMSOL) Sensor Design

Embedded Electronics for Sensors

Developed portable electronic interface systems for flexible strain and gas sensors, including LabVIEW-based test beds, IoT-enabled systems, and wireless control interfaces.

Embedded Systems LabVIEW IoT Python
